Videos About Resume

Raised in Phoenix, I first delved into film my sophomore year along with Youth and Government.


I learned to express myself through content and became passionate about sharing my views. My passion developed with every film video created with my my friends. That passion then transformed into making videos for non-profits. My first advertisement video was one made with a freind for a charter school called Vista College Preparatory in an impoverished part of Phoenix. Afterwards, I created a video for the YMCA's Triangle Awards event. That then led me to a job at the YMCA as a videogrpaher and cinematographer.


My passion for film was also largely influenced by Youth and Government. I not only want to make films, but I want to make films that matter. That is why I have worked with nonprofits, and hope to make more creative films that have an impact.
